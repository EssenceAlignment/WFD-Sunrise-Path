About This Assessment

This survey uses the Organizational Readiness for Implementing Change (ORIC) framework to assess manager readiness for the new data dashboard system.

  • 12 validated ORIC questions
  • 5-7 minutes to complete
  • Benchmark score: 3.5 or higher indicates readiness
  • Anonymous submission available
