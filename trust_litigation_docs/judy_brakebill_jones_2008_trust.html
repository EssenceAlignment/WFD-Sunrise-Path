THE JUDY BRAKEBILL JONES
2008 REVOCABLE TRUST
This Revocable Living Trust Agreement is made this 18th day of March, 2008, by JUDY LEE JONES, also known as JUDY BRAKEBILL JONES, JUDY BRAKEBILL JONES LASHER, or JUDY LEE JONES LASHER, of Monterey County, California, hereinafter referred to as "Grantor" or "Trustee."
At the time of its creation, the Grantor was married to Lawrence Elliot Lasher. The Trust is to be administered under the laws of the State of California.
ARTICLE I
CREATION OF TRUST
The Grantor hereby creates a revocable living trust to be known as "The Judy Brakebill Jones 2008 Revocable Trust" (hereinafter "Trust"). The Grantor hereby transfers, assigns, and conveys to the Trustee the property described in Schedule A attached hereto and incorporated herein by reference, together with any and all property hereafter transferred to the Trustee to be held, administered, and distributed according to the terms of this Trust Agreement.
ARTICLE II
PURPOSE OF TRUST
The purpose of this Trust is to receive and manage assets for the benefit of the Grantor during her lifetime and to further manage and distribute the assets of the Trust upon the death of the Grantor.
ARTICLE III
BENEFICIARIES
A. Primary Beneficiary: During the lifetime of the Grantor, the Grantor shall be the sole beneficiary of this Trust.
B. Remainder Beneficiaries: Upon the death of the Grantor, the Trust estate shall be distributed to the following beneficiaries as set forth in Article VI:
1. LAWRENCE ELLIOT LASHER
2. ERIC BRAKEBILL JONES
3. HEIDI MARICHEN JONES BLANCHARD
4. GRETCHEN ANGELA JONES
ARTICLE IV
ADMINISTRATION DURING GRANTOR'S LIFETIME
During the lifetime of the Grantor:
A. The Trustee shall pay to or apply for the benefit of the Grantor so much of the net income and principal of the Trust estate as the Grantor may request or as the Trustee determines necessary for the Grantor's health, education, support, and maintenance.
B. Any net income not distributed shall be accumulated and added to principal.
C. The Trustee shall have full power and authority to manage, invest, and reinvest the Trust estate.
ARTICLE V
INCAPACITY OF GRANTOR
If the Grantor becomes incapacitated, as determined by two licensed physicians, the successor Trustee shall assume the duties of Trustee and shall manage the Trust estate for the benefit of the Grantor, applying such amounts of income and principal as necessary for the Grantor's care, support, and maintenance.
ARTICLE VI
DISTRIBUTION UPON DEATH OF GRANTOR
Upon the death of the Grantor, the Trustee shall distribute the Trust estate as follows:
A. Pay all valid debts, expenses of last illness, funeral expenses, and expenses of administration.
B. Distribute the remaining Trust estate in equal shares to:
1. LAWRENCE ELLIOT LASHER
2. ERIC BRAKEBILL JONES
3. HEIDI MARICHEN JONES BLANCHARD
4. GRETCHEN ANGELA JONES
C. If any beneficiary predeceases the Grantor, that beneficiary's share shall be distributed to their then-living descendants, per stirpes. If no descendants survive, the share shall be divided equally among the surviving beneficiaries.
ARTICLE VII
TRUSTEE POWERS
The Trustee shall have all powers granted by California law and specifically including, but not limited to:
A. To invest and reinvest Trust assets
B. To buy, sell, lease, or mortgage real property
C. To borrow money and encumber Trust property
D. To operate any business interest
E. To employ agents, attorneys, and advisors
F. To make distributions in cash or in kind
G. To allocate receipts and disbursements between income and principal
ARTICLE VIII
AMENDMENT AND REVOCATION
The Grantor reserves the right to amend or revoke this Trust in whole or in part at any time during her lifetime by written instrument delivered to the Trustee. Upon revocation, the Trustee shall deliver all Trust property to the Grantor.
ARTICLE IX
SUCCESSOR TRUSTEE
A. The Grantor shall serve as the initial Trustee of this Trust.
B. Upon the death, incapacity, or resignation of the Grantor as Trustee, the following shall serve as successor Trustee in the order named:
1. ERIC BRAKEBILL JONES
2. HEIDI MARICHEN JONES BLANCHARD
ARTICLE X
TRUSTEE COMPENSATION
The Grantor, while serving as Trustee, shall receive no compensation. Any successor Trustee shall be entitled to reasonable compensation for services rendered.
ARTICLE XI
NO CONTEST CLAUSE
If any beneficiary contests this Trust or any of its provisions, any gift or interest given to that contesting beneficiary under this Trust is revoked and shall be disposed of as if that contesting beneficiary had predeceased the Grantor without descendants.
ARTICLE XII
GOVERNING LAW
This Trust shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.
ARTICLE XIII
SEVERABILITY
If any provision of this Trust is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue to be valid and enforceable.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the undersigned has executed this Trust Agreement on the date first written above.

Judy Lee Jones

JUDY LEE JONES, Grantor and Trustee
