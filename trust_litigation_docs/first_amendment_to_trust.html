FIRST AMENDMENT TO THE
JUDY BRAKEBILL JONES 2008 REVOCABLE TRUST
This First Amendment to the Judy Brakebill Jones 2008 Revocable Trust is made this 13th day of July, 2016, in Santa Clara County, California.
RECITALS:
WHEREAS, the undersigned, JUDY LEE JONES, also known as JUDY BRAKEBILL JONES, created that certain revocable trust known as the "Judy Brakebill Jones 2008 Revocable Trust" (hereinafter "Trust"); and
WHEREAS, the Grantor reserved the right to amend the Trust pursuant to Article VIII of the Trust; and
WHEREAS, the Grantor now desires to amend certain provisions of the Trust;
NOW, THEREFORE, the Trust is hereby amended as follows:
1. CLARIFICATION OF GRANTOR'S NAME: For all purposes of the Trust, it is hereby clarified that the Grantor's legal name is JUDY LEE JONES, who may also be known as JUDY BRAKEBILL JONES.
2. AMENDMENT TO ARTICLE VI: Article VI of the Trust is hereby amended to provide that the real property commonly known as 17742 Berta Canyon Road, Salinas, California 93907, shall be distributed in equal shares as follows:

One-fourth (1/4) to LAWRENCE ELLIOT LASHER

One-fourth (1/4) to ERIC BRAKEBILL JONES

One-fourth (1/4) to HEIDI MARICHEN JONES BLANCHARD

One-fourth (1/4) to GRETCHEN ANGELA JONES

3. SUCCESSOR TRUSTEE: Upon the death of the Settlor, ERIC BRAKEBILL JONES shall act as sole Trustee of the Trust.
4. SCHEDULE A: Schedule A attached to the original Trust is hereby replaced with the Amended Schedule A attached hereto and incorporated herein by this reference.
5. RATIFICATION: Except as specifically amended herein, all other provisions of the Trust shall remain in full force and effect.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the undersigned has executed this First Amendment to the Judy Brakebill Jones 2008 Revocable Trust on the date first written above.

Judy Lee Jones

JUDY LEE JONES, Trustor and Trustee
